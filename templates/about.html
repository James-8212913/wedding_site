Kids and Pets:
Due to the size of the venue we respectfully ask that no children attend. The venue is in a National Park which means that no animals are allowed (except party animals)

Getting there and Away:
Athol Hall is down near Taronga Zoo in Mosman in a National Park near a residential area. There are a few ways to get there and it depends where you are coming from:
  • From the CBD: The Ferry to the zoo is the way to go with a short (10 min walk) to the venue. A taxi from the CBD will cost around $45
  • From Manly: Unfortunately the Ferry doesn't go from Manly to the Zoo so you'll have to go to the city and get a ferry back to the Zoo and walk (10 mins). A taxi from Manly will cost $50
  • Driving: There is limited Parking near the venue, it is metered parking in the national park
  • Getting Away: An Uber or Taxi will is the best bet at the end of the night - it's a loooooong walk home from there....
